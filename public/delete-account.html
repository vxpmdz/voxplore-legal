Delete your Voxplore account

This page explains how users can request the deletion of their Voxplore account and associated personal data.

Delete your account from the app

  1. Open the Voxplore app
  2. Go to Profile → Settings → Privacy
  3. Select "Delete account"
  4. Confirm the deletion

Delete your account from the web

If you cannot access the app, you can request account deletion by contacting us at:

soporte@voxploreapp.com

Data deletion

When an account is deleted, Voxplore permanently removes all personal data associated with the user, including authentication data and tour usage history.
